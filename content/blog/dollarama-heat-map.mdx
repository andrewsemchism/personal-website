Dollarama is the only place I know where you can get a pair of shoes for under $5 in Ontario. It's got to be one of my all time favourite stores.

I vibe coded [this heat map](/vibes/dollarama-heat-map) with every Dollarama location in Canada (1700+ locations). It turns out you're _almost_ never more than 50km away from a Dollarama in southern Ontario! The areas in red represent areas with no Dollarama within 50km.
